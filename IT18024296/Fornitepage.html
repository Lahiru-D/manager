    Fortnite System Requirements (2020) - full specs and the gaming PC setup you need: Can I Run Fortnite?


    These are the Fortnite system requirements (Recommended)

    CPU: Core i5 2.8 Ghz
    RAM: 8 GB
    OS: Windows 7/8/10 64-bit
    VIDEO CARD: Nvidia GTX 660 or AMD Radeon HD 7870 equivalent DX11 GPU
    PIXEL SHADER: 5.0
    VERTEX SHADER: 5.0
    DEDICATED VIDEO RAM: 2048 MB
